Sec. 01 — About Me

Ronald Raphael Dumlao

Aerospace Engineer — Small Satellite A.I.T & Manufacturing / Systems Engineering Experience / Leadership

I am a recent graduate from California Polytechnic University of Pomona with my Bachelor's of Science Degree in Aerospace Engineering. I have a deep passion for space exploration, technological advancement, and turning concept into functional models through engineering design, testing, and improvement.

In university, I gained experience in solid rocket propulsion design, 3D design and GD&T prints of rocket motor casings, and most recently small satellite flight hardware additive manufacturing and subsystem assembly, integration, & testing (A.I.T). Generally, I enjoy performing "hands-on" work the most, working with tools, physical hardware, and assembling parts into functional machines.

Through the NASA L'SPACE Proposal Writing and Mission Concept Academies, I was able to improve and develop leadership and systems engineering skills through rigorous, team-oriented work-force training driven by real NASA standards, while using industry supported methods and software.
